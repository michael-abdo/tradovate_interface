# CME NQ Weekly Options Research
## Date: July 16, 2025

### Key Findings from Web Search:

## NQ Weekly Options at CME:
- **Monday and Wednesday Weekly Options**: Available on E-mini Nasdaq-100 futures
- **Tuesday and Thursday Weekly Options**: Also available
- **Style**: European-style options
- **Expiration**: 4:00 p.m. ET using volume-weighted average fixing price (NQF)

## Critical Discovery:
**Wednesday July 16, 2025 Analysis:**
- CME offers NQ weekly options expiring on Wednesdays
- The Q3C format (Week 3, Wednesday) was searched but not confirmed in official CME documentation
- Search results indicate NQ weekly options DO exist for Wednesday expirations

## Symbol Format Research:
- General CME weekly options format: symbol + week of the month + month code + year
- Specific Q3C/Q3D format not found in official CME documentation
- This suggests either:
  1. Q3C format is vendor-specific (not CME's native format)
  2. Different symbol convention used by Databento
  3. Options may not exist for this specific Wednesday
